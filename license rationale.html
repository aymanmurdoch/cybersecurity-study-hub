License Rationale

I have chosen the Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY) license for my Cybersecurity Study Hub project. This license allows others to share and adapt the materials provided, as long as proper credit is given. The aim is to encourage the broader cybersecurity community to contribute to and expand the hub while ensuring that the original creators are acknowledged. This open-source approach supports collaboration and continuous growth of the educational content, making it more accessible to individuals and organizations interested in learning and improving cybersecurity practices.
